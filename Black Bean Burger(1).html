BBB: Black Bean Burger

A Guide To Making Homemade Black Bean Burgers

Prep Time: 15 min

Cooking Time: 20 min

Ready In: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1 (16oz) can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ green bell pepper
  • ½ yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of chili powder
  • 1 tbsp of cumin
  • 1 tbsp of hot sauce
  • ½ cup of bread crumbs
  • salt

Things You'll Need

  • fork
  • knives and cutting board
  • pan or baking sheet
  • large bowl for mixing

Directions

  1. If grilling, preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil a sheet of aluminum foil. If baking, preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C), and lightly oil a baking sheet.
  2. In a medium bowl, mash black beans with a fork until thick and pasty.
  3. Finely chop bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Then stir into mashed beans.
  4. In a small bowl, stir together egg, chili powder, cumin, and chili sauce.
  5. Stir the egg mixture into the mashed beans. Mix in bread crumbs until the mixture is sticky and holds together. Divide mixture into four patties.
  6. If grilling, place patties on foil, and grill about 8 minutes on each side. If baking, place patties on baking sheet, and bake about 10 minutes on each side.
  7. Add avocado, spinach, tomato, cheese or any toppings you would usually put on a burger
  8. Put that bad boy on a bun and call it a day!
